About me

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in Multimedia Lab (MMLab)(established by Prof. Xiaoou Tang), the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang. I also work very closely with Dr. Ziwei Liu and Prof. Ping Luo.

I received my bachelor's degree in Acoustics from the School of Physics at Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017. My research interests include deep learning and its applications on video and audio processing.
